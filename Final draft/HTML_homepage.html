The Mirror of Mirrors

A Literary Anthology in Honour of the Mirror

This site brings together a collection of extracts from classical literature which deal with or mention the mirror. Speculum Speculorum pays homage to mirrors and the writers who wrote about them. It also serves to provide a bibliography of critical texts that consider the use of the mirror as a literary device, for those interested in the mirror in an academic context.
